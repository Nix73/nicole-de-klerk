I've made my home in the beautiful Southern Peninsula of Cape Town. After a year travelling through Indonesia, Vietnam, Singapore, Panama, Chile and the USA I came back feeling inspired to change careers and decided that Frontend development could be that possibilty.

Now I’ve made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
